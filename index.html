Meet our founder.

Leean Ramon Orama, CGC.

Hailing from a drywall family my jobsite debut came early at age 5. Every summer was a different site until attending my hometown Newark’s NJIT. Here I got my Bachelor’s Science in Engineering Technology - Concrete Industries Management, minoring in Business 2010.

The next 15 years of commercial contracting experience comprise a humbling project portfolio delivering projects in NYC & Tampa for prime commercial clients in class A offices, out of ground hi-rise, residential mid-rise, airport, national retail franchises, industrial, & University/K-12, among others.

Some career highlights include working on Freedom Tower WTC1, Novartis Building 337, JFK Terminal 4, 900 Monroe Hoboken, & the LinkNYC kiosk network.

Our commitment to building excellence demands providing you invaluable construction expertise, diligence, & impeccable service. We’re looking forward to discussing your next project!
